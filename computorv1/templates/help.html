This is Computor V1 for the programming school 42



The project is about resolving polynomial equation until degree 2 !

You can get the french subject



How to

Enter some equation in the two fields in the main page :

5 * X^0 + 4 * X^1 - 9.3 * X^2 = 0


or something more natural like :

5 + 4x - 9,3 x**2


When only one field is filled, the second one takes 0 by default

Bonus

Steps displayed
  • Given Equation
  • Formatted Equation (if different from given)
  • Reduced form
  • Degree
  • Discriminant (if degree is 2)
  • Solutions







    • Natural form

    Equation can also given with natural form

  • 4          4 * X^0
  • x         1 * X^1
  • 5x       5 * X^1
  • x*5      5 * X^1





    • Easy form

    Equation can also given with easy form

  • x**2      X^2
  • 00.0     0
  • 01        1
  • ,           .
  • add missing spaces
  • remove useless spaces







    • Fraction

    When the following conditions are satisfied, results are displayed as fractions:

  • numerator <= 10
  • denominator <= 100



    • Terminal options

    For terminal using the following options are available:

  • goodies (formatting & colorize) -g True
  • don't catch the exception -e True
